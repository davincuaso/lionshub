Baliklaya

baliklayaorganization@gmail.com

Rebuilding society one citizen at a time.

What is Baliklaya and what do we do?

  • Create opportunities for interaction with persons deprived of liberty
  • Foster critical understanding and reflection on citizens’ rights and responsibilities
  • Help raise awareness for current issues in the prison sector and the justice system.

Why should people join Baliklaya?

  • Rarely will you find a student organization that engages the prison sector, and grounds its members in shared experiences with prisoners. Take the chance to share stories with kuyas from the New Bilibid Prison. Be part of the change in their lives, and realize why their reformation is everyone’s responsibility. We’re rebuilding society one citizen at a time.

Vision

We envision an inclusive Filipino society free from prejudice which produces dignified and productive citizens for the progress of the nation.

Mission

Promote the value of life, dignity and rights of every human through experiences with persons deprived of liberty
