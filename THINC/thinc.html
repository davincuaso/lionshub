THINC

Tabletop, Hobbyists, & Interests Collaborative

Extrathincorg@gmail.com

TTHINC advocates the novelty and fun of playing tabletop games and brings people together through this shared passion. Whether you’re a hardcore enthusiast, a casual hobbyist, or a curious newcomer, there’s always room at our table for a new player.

What is THINC and what do we do?

  • Here at THINC, we foster a community of hobbyists interested in tabletop activities such as card games, board games, wargaming, and tabletop role-playing games. We provide our members with a place where they can play games with their friends, exercise their brains by encouraging strategy and creativity, and take a break from the stressful motions of everyday life. Our goal is to keep the tradition of tabletop gaming alive and promote it as a fun, healthy, and productive activity for everyone.

Why should people join THINC?

  • THINC is about fun and community. We do our best to give our members activities to enjoy themselves and celebrate the value of gathering your friends to have a great time playing games together. It may seem simple, but every game you play is an opportunity for unforgettable memories.

Vision

A leading community for interested individuals to openly delve and pursue into hobbies of choice under the guidance, tutelage, and camaraderie of welcoming old and new enthusiasts; where people advocate the positives of these interests particularly in the psychological benefits, social utility, cultural relevance and trends, fine arts, and national identity.

Mission

To organize a proper place for each hobby and interest to be appreciated. Our mission is to produce a social atmosphere where people can freely share hobbies and interests with respect and camaraderie towards each other. This keeps in mind strategic and analytic thinking, interpersonal capacity, and personal growth for every member while maintaining healthy relationships with neighboring and interested communities.
