UpStart Loyola Schools

upstartateneo@gmail.com

UpStart-LS is a student organization that aims to empower students through career opportunities with Startups.

What is UpStart and what do we do?

  • We bridge between students and the local startup scene.
  • We encourage appreciation and recognition for the local startup scene.
  • We expose students to the innovation in the local startup scene.

Why should people join UpStart?

  • Upstart-LS provides exposure and appreciation of startups to the students. Our organization’s flat structure encourages students to step-up and make valuable changes to the organization.

Vision

Our end goal as an organization is to open students’ eyes that working in startups are a better alternative to the corporate career.

Mission

Upstart-LS aims to be the bridge that connects and orients students to startups and the local startup scene, while at the same time creating a community with a passion for business and solving problems. These objectives are achieved by having initiative-based projects and solid partnerships with the local startups in the industry.
