About LIONS

With the goal of striving for equality and justice for independent organizations of the University, the League of Independent Organizations (LIONS) strives to protect the rights and welfare of its constituent organizations. LIONS forwards the interests of its member organizations, represents and defends them in official student and University bodies, and provides support and information crucial to their development. LIONS was officially founded in 2010. The first LIONS RecWeek was held in 2012.

LIONS is proud to have a wide array of organizations with diverse purposes, advocacies, principles and core competencies. LIONS is an inclusive community that is home to passionate and driven people. These organizations are not affiliated with the Council of Organizations of Ateneo. These organizations represent the Council, and work hand-in-hand with the Coordinating Body to ensure no organization gets left behind.

The Council is composed of the highest executive officer of each constituent organization and sets the direction of LIONS. The Coordinating Body, composed of the Coordinator, Student Representative, University Representative, Secretary General, and Director for Internal Affairs, carries out the relevant tasks and activities of the League.

We believe every organization deserves the opportunity to realize itself to the fullest. We are courageous, strong, and steadfast. We are LIONS.
