Join LIONS Organizations

Step 1: Visit LIONS RecWeek 2017 at Colayco Pavilion on August 29-31, 2017 between 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM.

Step 2: Check out all 29 organizations! You are more than welcome to swing by the booths of organizations you’re interested in and talk to the member at the booth to get to know the organization more. Don’t be afraid to ask questions!

Step 3: Once you’ve chosen which organization you’re joining, simply go to their booth and register! Registration only takes around 1 minute. Then pay the registration fee, if applicable (PHP 100 or PHP 80 if you're a scholar).

Step 4: Join as many organizations as you wish.

Step 5: You’re done! Welcome to LIONS!
